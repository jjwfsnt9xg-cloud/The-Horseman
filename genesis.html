I do not train horses

I work with them until they no longer feel the need to resist

The Work

For over eleven years, I have worked with horses in a way that does not rely on force, domination, or mechanical repetition. What I do is not about control. It is about understanding the horse in the moment.

The Shift

Instead of asking how to make a horse obey, I began asking why it resists. That change shaped everything I do today.

How I Work

I reduce pressure, observe behaviour, and allow the horse to process. The goal is not obedience. The goal is clarity.

Experience

Stallions, reactive horses, and unstable behaviours are not problems to fix. They are patterns to understand.
