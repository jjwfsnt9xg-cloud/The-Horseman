The Author Behind the Journal

I Did Not Choose Horses —
They Chose Me

Eleven years of silence, surrender, and presence.

This journal was not planned. It emerged from eleven years spent in the company of horses — learning their language, their patience, their refusal to lie. I came looking for a skill. I found a philosophy.

I am not a trainer, not a competitor. I am a student of relationship — of what happens when two beings meet with full honesty and no agenda. Every horse I've known has taught me something I couldn't have learned from a book: about presence, about trust, about the courage it takes to truly listen.

"The horse does not care what you know. It cares who you are in this moment — and whether you are willing to be fully here."
